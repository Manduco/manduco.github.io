About Me

I spend a lot of time playing with code, designing and drinking coffee. I have a passion for Automation, UX/UI and Databases.

Why Automation? A great professor once told me that the best developers, creatives, and artists are all lazy, in a sense. They work smarter not harder by developing faster workflow through means of efficiency.

Why UX/UI? I love making something people will see and enjoy using, like art but functional.

Why Databases? There is this beauty behind the scenes in databases. Being able to compile, analyze and draw insight from simple data is a gratifying process for me.

Why do I Code? I enjoy coding because it’s a way to express my creativity while problem-solving.

Who Am I? A passionate person with a desire to solve and create.
